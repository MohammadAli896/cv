What is a Robot?

  • A robot is a machine that is able to replicate certain human movements and functions automatically.
  • A robot can be assigned multiple programs or functions.

An example of what a Robot can accomplish:

  1. Grab a piece of toast.
  2. Grab the butter.
  3. Grab a butterknife and spread the butter on the bread.
